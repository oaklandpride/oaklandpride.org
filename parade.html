The Heartbeat of Oakland: The 2025 Pride Parade!

This September, the streets of Oakland will once again pulse with the vibrant colors of love, resistance, and radical joy. Join us as we march, dance, and cheer at the 15th Annual Oakland Pride Parade!

This isn't just a parade; it's a powerful declaration. It's a celebration of every identity, a testament to our community's resilience, and a promise to continue fighting for a future where everyone is safe, seen, and celebrated. From the roar of the Dykes on Bikes to the rhythmic steps of our community dancers, every moment is a piece of our collective story.

In Unity, We Thrive!

This year's theme, "In Unity, We Thrive!", is a call to action. It's a recognition that our strength comes from our diversity and our power comes from standing together. We invite you, your friends, your family, and your allies to join us as we embody this theme and fill the streets of The Town with an undeniable message of solidarity.

Parade Details

Date: Sunday, September 7, 2025

Time: The Parade kicks off at 11:00 AM sharp!

New Route: This year, we're reversing the route! The parade will still march down Broadway, culminating in a grand finale at our new festival location surrounding Oakland City Hall (14th & Broadway).

Cost: Watching the parade is completely FREE for everyone! Come early to find a great spot and cheer on the contingents.
