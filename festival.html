Festival Details

Date: Sunday, September 7, 2025

Time: 11:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Location: The festival is located in the heart of the city, surrounding the iconic Oakland City Hall at 14th & Broadway.

Tickets: You'll need a ticket to enter the festival grounds. To save time, we highly encourage you to purchase your tickets in advance!

Getting Here

Parking will be extremely limited, so we strongly recommend using public transportation.

BART: The 12th St. Oakland City Center and 19th St. Oakland stations are both just a short walk from the festival entrance. This is the easiest way to get to the celebration.

AC Transit: Numerous bus lines serve the downtown Oakland area. Check the AC Transit website for routes and schedules.

Ride-Sharing: If using a ride-sharing service, set your drop-off point a few blocks away from the festival grounds to avoid traffic congestion.

What to Bring / What to Leave at Home To ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone, please review the following:

YES! Please Bring

  • Your ticket (digital or printed)
  • A reusable water bottle (stay hydrated at our free water stations!)
  • Sunscreen, a hat, and sunglasses
  • Comfortable shoes (you'll be doing a lot of dancing!)
  • Your best Pride outfit and positive energy
  • Credit/debit cards (many vendors will be cashless)

NO. Please Leave at Home

  • Outside alcohol or glass containers
  • Weapons of any kind
  • Large bags or backpacks (all bags are subject to search)
  • Drones or professional recording equipment (unless you have a press pass)
  • Pets (service animals are welcome)
