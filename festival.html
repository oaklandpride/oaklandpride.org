What to Expect at Oakland Pride Festival

Live Entertainment

Multiple stages featuring incredible performances from local and national LGBTQ+ artists, DJs, and performers.

Diverse Vendors

Browse unique offerings from LGBTQ+ owned businesses, artisans, and community organizations.

Food & Drinks

Enjoy delicious food and refreshing beverages from local restaurants and beverage vendors.

Community Connection

Meet local LGBTQ+ organizations, resources, and connect with your community in a safe, celebratory space.

Getting to Oakland Pride

We encourage you to use public transportation to reach the festival.

BART

Downtown Oakland BART stations provide easy access to the festival area. Details on closest stations will be announced with the venue location.

AC Transit

Multiple bus lines serve downtown Oakland. Check AC Transit for routes and schedules closer to the event date.

Ride-Share & Parking

Parking is limited downtown. Ride-share drop-off locations will be announced. Consider sustainable transportation!

Festival Preparation Guide

YES! Please Bring

  • Your festival ticket (digital or printed)
  • Reusable water bottle (free water stations available)
  • Sunscreen, hat, and sunglasses
  • Comfortable shoes for dancing
  • Your best Pride outfit & positive energy
  • Credit/debit cards (many vendors are cashless)
  • Valid ID (21+ for alcohol areas)

NO - Please Leave at Home

  • Outside alcohol or glass containers
  • Weapons of any kind
  • Large bags or backpacks (all bags subject to search)
  • Drones or professional recording equipment
  • Pets (service animals welcome)
  • Illegal substances
  • Discrimination, hate, or negativity
