Welcome, neighbor! The annual Oakland Pride Parade and Festival is a celebration of our city's diversity, unity, and spirit. To ensure a safe and joyful event for everyone, some street closures and parking restrictions will be in effect. We appreciate your patience and cooperation!

### Event Details

* **Event:** Oakland Pride Parade & Festival
* **Date:** Sunday, September 7, 2025
* **Parade Time:** 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM
* **Festival Time:** 11:00 AM - 7:00 PM

---

### Street Closures & Parking Restrictions üöß

For the safety of participants and attendees, the following streets will be closed to all vehicle traffic. **Parking will be strictly prohibited**, and vehicles will be towed at the owner's expense beginning early Sunday morning.

**Closure Times:** Sunday, September 7, from **6:00 AM to 10:00 PM** (unless otherwise noted).

**Parade Route Closures (6:00 AM - 2:00 PM):**
* **Broadway** from 14th St to 20th St
* **20th St** from Broadway to Harrison St

**Festival Area Closures (6:00 AM - 10:00 PM):**
* **Franklin St** from 19th St to 22nd St
* **20th St** from Webster St to Harrison St
* **21st St** from Webster St to Harrison St

### Important Information for Residents

#### Parking & Towing üöó

If you are parked within the closure zones, please move your vehicle before **5:00 AM on Sunday, September 7**.

**Towing will begin promptly at 5:00 AM.** If your vehicle has been towed, please contact the official police non-emergency line at `(510) 777-3333` to find its location. We strongly advise moving your vehicle on Saturday evening to avoid any issues.

#### Traffic & Getting Around

Expect significant traffic delays in and around downtown Oakland throughout the day. We highly recommend using public transportation, biking, or planning alternate routes that avoid the event area.

#### Public Transit üöå

AC Transit bus lines that normally run through the area will be rerouted. Please check the **[AC Transit website](https://www.actransit.org/alerts)** for a complete list of detours and temporary bus stops.

---

### Questions or Concerns?

For non-emergency questions about the event or street closures, you can contact the Oakland Pride organizing committee at `info@oaklandpride.org`. For any immediate public safety concerns, please call the non-emergency police line. **In an emergency, always dial 911.**
