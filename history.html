--- layout: page title: Oakland Pride History description: Learn about the rich history of Oakland Pride and its impact on the community. ---

Oakland Pride has a rich history that dates back to its founding in 1997. Since then, it has grown into one of the most vibrant and inclusive Pride celebrations in the Bay Area.

The event was created to celebrate the LGBTQ+ community in Oakland and to provide a safe space for individuals to express themselves and connect with others.

Over the years, Oakland Pride has faced various challenges, including funding issues and the need for greater visibility within the community. However, it has persevered and continues to thrive, thanks to the support of local organizations and community members.
