## **Oakland Pride Turns 15 — Bigger, Bolder, and Louder Than Ever\!**

*New festival location, reversed parade route, superstar lineup, and a celebration of community, culture, and Town Rainbow Pride.*

**OAKLAND, CA —** Mark your calendars — **Oakland Pride 2025** is pulling out all the stops for its **15th Anniversary Celebration** on **Sunday, September 7, 2025**, from **11:00 AM to 6:00 PM**. This year brings **a brand-new festival location at Frank H. Ogawa Plaza**, directly in front of Oakland City Hall, and a **reversed parade route** kicking off at **22nd and Broadway**.

From the streets to the stages, this year’s Pride is all about celebrating the heartbeat of the Bay — our people, our culture, and our unstoppable spirit.

### **STAR POWER THAT MOVES YOU**

The Oakland LGBTQI+ Community Center Main Stage will feature powerhouse performances from **Martha Wash**, **CeCe Peniston**, and Oakland’s own **Kamaiyah**.  
The Latin Stage will shine with **international icon Christian Chávez**, while the brand-new *Mighty Real* House Music Stage — curated by the legendary **David Harness** — will keep the dance energy going all day long.

### **MORE THAN A PARTY — A CELEBRATION FOR EVERYONE**

Oakland Pride is for the entire community, featuring:

* **Kids & Family Area** – Safe, fun, and interactive spaces with games, activities, and entertainment for all ages.  
* **Food Delights Zone** – From local comfort food to global flavors, enjoy dishes from some of the Bay Area’s favorite chefs and vendors.  
* **Local Market & Community Booths** – Explore stalls from artisans, small businesses, and nonprofits representing the rich diversity of Oakland.

### **PARADE WITH PRIDE**

The 2025 Parade will spotlight celebrities and community champions including **W. Kamau Bell**, **Layshia Clarendon**, and **Patrik-Ian Polk**, along with community heroes **Tim Nguyen** and **Somos Familia Bay**.

*“This is more than a festival — it’s a movement,”* says **George Jeffery Smith III**, President of Oakland Pride. *“We’re here to celebrate 15 years of community, visibility, and love — and we’re just getting started.”*

**About Oakland Pride, Inc.**  
Oakland Pride, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to celebrating and uplifting the cultures and diversity of the LGBTQ+ community in Oakland and the East Bay. Through our annual festival, parade, and year-round community engagement, we promote equality, social justice, and unity for all.
