OAKLAND PRIDE!

In Unity, We Thrive

"In Unity, We Thrive" is a powerful theme that emphasizes the strength found in solidarity and collective action within the LGBTQ+ community and its allies. It celebrates the idea that by coming together, celebrating our diversity, and supporting one another, we can build a more vibrant and resilient community for all.

The Visit Oakland website offers a variety of promotions and deals for hotels, making it a great resource for planning a trip. You can find information on exclusive packages and discounts for various hotel types, ranging from chic boutique hotels to family-friendly stays. For example, some offers include limited-time deals such as a "Stay the Night, Ride on Us" promotion that provides a $25 Uber credit with a one-night hotel stay. Beyond the official tourism site, other platforms like Expedia, HotelTonight, and Priceline also feature numerous hotel deals in Oakland, including last-minute and member-exclusive savings, as well as vacation packages that bundle flights and accommodations.
