Passing the Torch

After more than 15 incredible years of celebrating the LGBTQ+ community, the nonprofit organization behind Oakland Pride is passing the leadership torch.

We are thrilled to announce that Oakland Pride 2026 will continue under the new leadership of the Oakland LGBTQ Community Center.

As the previous organization winds down operations, responses to info@oaklandpride.org may be limited or delayed. A new website for the 2026 event will launch when details are available.
